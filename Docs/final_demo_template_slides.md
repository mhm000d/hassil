## Problem slide

The Work Is Done. The Money Isn’t There.
Freelancers and small businesses in the Arab region do not only struggle to earn money, they struggle to access it on time.

A business may deliver a project, issue an invoice, and still wait 30–60+ days to get paid. A freelancer may finish a job, but the money can remain stuck in platform review, escrow, security holds, or withdrawal processing.

During that gap, life does not wait. Salaries, rent, suppliers, taxes, tools, and bills are due before the money arrives.

Then add 2 small stat boxes:
63% of MENA MSMEs lack access to finance
$210–240B MSME financing gap in MENA

Banks are not built for this small, short-term cash-flow gap. Many SMEs lack collateral, credit history, or access to formal finance, even when they have real invoices and expected income.

The money was earned. It just hasn’t arrived yet. That is the problem we are solving.

## Solution Slide

Hassil — Get Paid When the Work Is Done.

Hassil turns confirmed work into usable cash in hours not weeks.
Eliminating long bank process, collateral-first approval, and compounding interest. Just a confirmed invoice, a clear flat fee, and money when it is actually needed.

For SMBs
A business submits a confirmed invoice, the client verifies the work was delivered, Hassil advances up to 90% of the invoice value, and When the client pays later, Hassil collects the advance plus one flat fee, then releases the remaining balance.

For Freelancers
A freelancer creates or uploads an invoice and requests an advance, Hassil reviews the invoice, work history, and trust score, once approved, the freelancer receives up to 80% today, the client relationship stays private, and the freelancer repays Hassil after the client pays.

Hassil make it easy than before
* One platform, two models Invoice factoring for SMBs. Invoice discounting for freelancers.
* No interest-based pricing One upfront flat fee. No compounding. No hidden penalties.
* Built for the Arab market Designed around delayed payments, limited credit access, and trust-based business relationships.
* Gets better with trust Successful repayments unlock higher limits, faster approvals, and lower fees.

The money was earned. Hassil makes sure it arrives when it is needed.
